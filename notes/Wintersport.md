I did some research into the ski area's by looking at wintersport.nl. I wrote some keypoints below for each area.
Les Arcs:
+ Big Ski Area with a lot of variety.
+ Focussed on more advanced skiers (not recommended for beginners by wintersport.nl, however many comments disagree)
+ Expensive Restaurants and supermarkets
+ Very busy.
Val Cenis:
* According to comments the area is well suited for beginners.
* Relatively cheap restaurants and supermarkets
* Not so many challenging pistes
* Not so busy as other ski resorts.
* Not so much variety. (all pistes look similar)
* Easy to reach
Val thorens:
* Good apres ski
* Very large area (Enough for advanced and beginners)
* Nice views
* (Absurdly) Expensive restaurants and supermarkets
* Can be very cold,icy and windy because of height, but snow guaranteed.
* Somewhat more difficult to reach.
Tignes:
* Large area
* Big pistes
* Lifts are a bit old
* Great views from the pistes
